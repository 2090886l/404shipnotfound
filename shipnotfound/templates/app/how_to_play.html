How to Play

Object of the Game

Be the first to sink all 5 of your opponent’s ships

Contents

There are two grids, defensive and offensive grids. The defensive grid is your grid with your boats laid out. The Offensive grid is the grid you are attacking; the aim is to guess where your opponent’s ships are.

For a hit For a miss For not bombed

On the right hand side is the “Captain’s Log” which logs the shots taken by you and your opponent.

How to Play

You and your opponent will alternate turns, with each turn being logged in the “Captain’s Log”. Simply click on one of your opponent’s locations to bomb that location. Each player has 5 ships, one ship length 5, one ship length 4, two ships length 3 and one ship length 2.

Winning the Game

If you sink your opponent’s entire fleet of 5 ships before their sink your fleet, then you win the game.
